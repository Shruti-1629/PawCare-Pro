Turtle Care Guide

Habitat & Tank Setup

Nutrition & Diet

1. Feed a mix of commercial turtle pellets and leafy greens.

2. Offer occasional live food like insects or small fish.

3. Remove uneaten food to avoid water contamination.

4. Calcium supplements improve shell strength.

5. Monitor appetite and weight regularly.

Health & Hygiene

1. Regularly clean tank and basking areas.

2. Watch for shell rot or fungal infections.

3. Ensure UV light exposure for vitamin D3.

4. Quarantine new turtles before adding them to the tank.

5. Consult a vet if lethargy or abnormal behavior occurs.
