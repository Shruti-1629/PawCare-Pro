Learn to keep your colorful parrot healthy, smart, and social.

Parrot Care Guide

Cage & Habitat

1. Use a spacious cage for movement and flight.

2. Include perches of varying sizes for foot health.

3. Keep cage clean to prevent infections.

4. Avoid direct sunlight and drafts.

5. Provide toys for mental stimulation.

Nutrition & Feeding

1. Offer a mix of seeds, pellets, fruits, and vegetables.

2. Fresh water must be available at all times.

3. Avoid chocolate, caffeine, and avocado.

4. Provide cuttlebone for calcium.

5. Observe for selective eating or weight changes.

Socialization & Training

1. Interact with your parrot daily for bonding.

2. Teach simple commands using positive reinforcement.

3. Provide toys and puzzles for mental stimulation.

4. Rotate perches and toys for variety.

5. Monitor for stress or feather plucking.

Extra Tips For Parrot Owners

Ensure Cage Cleanliness
Provide UV Lighting
Feed Balanced Diet
Offer Daily Interaction
Monitor Behavior & Health
Rotate Toys Regularly
