Essential tips to keep your tiny friend active and healthy.

Hamster Care Guide

Housing & Environment

1. Use a spacious cage with tunnels and wheels.

2. Provide soft bedding for burrowing.

3. Keep cage away from direct sunlight and drafts.

4. Clean cage weekly to prevent odor and disease.

5. Offer hiding spots for safety and comfort.

Nutrition & Feeding

1. Provide hamster pellets as main diet.

2. Offer fresh vegetables and occasional fruits.

3. Avoid sugary or sticky foods.

4. Fresh water must always be available.

5. Monitor for signs of malnutrition or obesity.

Activity & Enrichment

1. Use exercise wheels and tunnels for physical activity.

2. Rotate toys to prevent boredom.

3. Handle gently to build trust.

4. Avoid loud noises that stress your hamster.

5. Observe daily behavior for health signs.

Extra Tips For Hamster Owners
