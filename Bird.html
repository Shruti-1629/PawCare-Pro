Guide to keeping your feathered friend healthy, happy, and active.

Bird Care Guide

Housing & Environment

1. Provide a spacious cage for flying and movement.

2. Ensure proper ventilation and sunlight exposure.

3. Keep cage clean to prevent infections.

4. Use perches of varying sizes for foot health.

5. Avoid drafts and sudden temperature changes.

Nutrition & Diet

1. Feed a mix of seeds, pellets, fruits, and vegetables.

2. Provide fresh water daily.

3. Avoid chocolate, avocado, and caffeine.

4. Offer cuttlefish bone for calcium.

5. Monitor for signs of obesity or malnutrition.

Socialization & Enrichment

1. Spend time talking or singing to your bird daily.

2. Provide toys to prevent boredom.

3. Allow supervised out-of-cage time.

4. Rotate toys for mental stimulation.

5. Observe behavior for stress signs.

Extra Tips For Bird Owners
