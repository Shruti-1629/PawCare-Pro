Tips for new cat owners to keep their feline friend happy and healthy.

Cat Care Guide

Daily Care Tips

1. Feed a balanced diet.

2. Provide fresh water.

3. Groom to prevent hairballs.

4. Keep litter box clean.

5. Create a safe, stress-free environment.

Health & Vet Visits

1. Schedule regular vet checkups.

2. Vaccinate as recommended.

3. Keep dental hygiene in check.

4. Observe behavioral changes.

5. Monitor for common infections.

Play & Mental Stimulation

1. Provide toys and scratching posts.

2. Interactive play daily.

3. Encourage climbing and exploration.

4. Reduce boredom-related behavior.

5. Ensure a quiet resting space.
