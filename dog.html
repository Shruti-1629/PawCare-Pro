Dog Care Guide

Daily Care Tips

1. Provide 30‚Äì60 minutes of daily exercise.

2. Feed a balanced diet for age and breed.

3. Keep fresh water accessible.

4. Groom regularly: brush, trim nails, bathe.

5. Check for ticks, fleas, and skin issues.

Training & Socialization

1. Start obedience training early.

2. Introduce your dog to people, pets, environments.

3. Use positive reinforcement.

4. Avoid harsh punishments.

5. Provide chew toys to prevent destructive behavior.

Health & Vaccination

1. Schedule regular vet checkups.

2. Maintain vaccinations and boosters.

3. Keep dental hygiene clean.

4. Watch for behavioral or physical changes.

5. Spaying/neutering is recommended.

Nutrition & Diet

1. Feed high-quality dog food.

2. Avoid chocolate, grapes, onions, fatty foods.

3. Offer fruits and vegetables as treats.

4. Measure portions to avoid obesity.

5. Maintain a feeding schedule.

Emergency Preparedness

1. Keep a first-aid kit.

2. Know the nearest 24-hour vet clinic.

3. Learn basic first aid: CPR, choking, wounds.

4. Avoid toxic chemicals at home.

5. Monitor for heatstroke or poisoning.
